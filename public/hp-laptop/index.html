HP PAVILION 15-N013TX BRIEF DESCRIPTION

HP Pavilion 15-n013TX laptop runs on Windows 8 (64 bit) OS and has Intel Core i5 (4th generation) 1.6 GHz with Intel Turbo boost up to 2.6Ghz processor. The laptop has Nvidia GeForce GT 740M 2GB DDR3 graphics processor. The HP Pavilion 15-n013TX notebook has a massive 1 TB HDD, an 8x SuperMulti DVD RW Drive with Dual Layer Support and 4 GB RAM for faster processing. It has a 15.6 inch HD BrightView LED-backlit Display with 1366 x 768 pixels resolution.
